Information and Referral

University catering in Padua - RistorESU - annually grants about 1,500,000 meals, addressing the entire student population of the University of Padua.

Students in other Italian or foreign universities, temporarily present in Padua for study, can access RistorESU upon request.

For information on your right of access to catering facilities please contact the Benefits Office and interventions:
