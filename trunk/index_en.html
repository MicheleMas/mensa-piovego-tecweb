Cafeteria "Nord Piovego"

The RistorESU "Nord Piovego" is located in Padova, Via G. Colombo, 1.

Restaurant, Pizzeria and Bar, adjacent to many faculties, by itself it produces about 500,000 meals every year.

It offers 700 seats and 6 distribution points.

In this RistorESU you can taste, in addition to the variety of traditional meals, pizzas, mixed salads and sandwiches.
