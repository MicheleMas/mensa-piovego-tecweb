New fees

02/09/2013

Have been approved the rules to access the college cafeteria A.Y. 2013/2014. The new tariffs implement the provisions of the Veneto Region with DGR n. 1011 of 18.06.2013, adapted to the rate of inflation and will come into force from September 2. The ranges of access concerned are A and B. The adjustment proposed by Istat proposed by ESU last May was not about the A range, because it is considered a weak category. The upgrade includes an increase of 20 cents for the band A, and band B is updated to 10 cents for the entire meal and 20 cents for the reduced meal. Instead remain unchanged rates for the menus "SpeedyEsu."
