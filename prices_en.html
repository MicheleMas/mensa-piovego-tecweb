General informations

Every student has the right to consume only one meal daily at noon and one meal in the evening. For offenders shall apply the sanctions provided for by law.

The card and paper vouchers are strictly personal, non-transferable and must be performed at the time of the enjoyment of the meal.In case of loss or theft of the card, in order to avoid misuse by third parties, the student must notify the Benefits Office and the subsequent deactivation.

ESU can request a sample, the performance of receipt and a valid identity document in order to verify the correspondence between the data shown on the card and the identity of the owner. The no-show of the card will result in the application of E rate.

Students enrolled in the first year, beneficiaries of scholarship in subsequent assignments at first, can ask ESU reimbursement of meals paid by 1 January 2014, addressing the Benefits and measures Office (mail: dirittoallostudio@esu.pd.it).
